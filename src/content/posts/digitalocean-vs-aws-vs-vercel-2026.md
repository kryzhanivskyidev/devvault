## AWS — The Industry Standard

AWS is the undisputed king of cloud infrastructure. With 200+ services and global reach, it powers everything from Netflix to NASA.

**Pros:**
- Unmatched service variety
- Global infrastructure
- Enterprise-grade reliability

**Cons:**
- Steep learning curve
- Complex, unpredictable billing
- Overkill for small projects

## Vercel — Frontend Developer's Dream

Vercel is purpose-built for frontend deployment, especially Next.js. If you're building JAMstack apps, Vercel's DX is hard to beat.

**Pros:**
- Zero-config deployments
- Incredible CI/CD pipeline
- Edge network out of the box
- Free tier is generous

**Cons:**
- Not suited for backend-heavy apps
- Gets expensive at scale
