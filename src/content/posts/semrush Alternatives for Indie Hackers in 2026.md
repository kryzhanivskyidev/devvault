Search Console is free and essential.

## Option 3: Ubersuggest

Ubersuggest is usually cheaper and easier to get into than Semrush. It is a reasonable option for smaller projects where you mainly need keyword ideas and light audits.

It is not as deep, but it is less intimidating.

## Option 4: Low-cost Workflow

If you want the leanest possible setup, combine:

- Google Search Console,
- Google Analytics,
- one paid SEO tool,
- manual internal linking.

That gives you enough signal to grow without overpaying for features you may never use.

## Who Should Skip Semrush

Semrush may be too much if you:

- only run one tiny site,
- do not need audits,
- do not track rankings,
- are not publishing often,
- want the cheapest possible setup.

In that case, use free tools until your site has momentum.
