Why Developers Need a VPN
--------------------------

- **Remote work on public WiFi** — coffee shops, co-working spaces, airports
- **Testing geo-restricted APIs** — some APIs behave differently by region
- **Accessing self-hosted services** — VPN as a lightweight alternative to a full VPN server
- **Protecting SSH sessions** — on untrusted networks
- **Bypassing corporate/ISP blocks** — during development and testing
